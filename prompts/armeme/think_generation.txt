**What is a Meme:**
A meme is an image with added text meant to convey humor, sarcasm, commentary, or a short clear message. Its meaning comes from the interaction between the image and the text.

Key properties:
1. Text added to the image.
2. The meaning emerges from image + text together.
3. Expressive faces, cultural symbols, or visual cues may reinforce the message.
4. Memes can critique, satirize, or highlight social/political issues.

**What is Propaganda:**
